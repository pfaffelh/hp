Lecturer: Prof. Dr. Peter Pfaffelhuber, 24.11.2026 – 22.12.2026.

Content: elementary probability without measure theory. We start with the basic models (coin tossing, dice, urns), random variables and combinatorial formulae, then treat independence, product spaces and conditional probability, discrete random variables with expectation and variance, and finally densities and the central limit theorem. A catalogue of the most important distributions is given in the appendix.
